# KVKK (Kișisel Verilerin Korunması Kanunu) Compliance Text

## 1. Introduction
This document explains how **Şeffaf Bağış Platformu** processes personal data in compliance with the Law on the Protection of Personal Data (KVKK) No. 6698.

## 2. Data Categories Processed
We process the following categories of data:
- **Identity Information**: Name, Surname, TC Identity Number.
- **Contact Information**: Email address, Phone number, Address.
- **Transaction Security**: IP addresses, Login logs, Password hashes.
- **Financial Information**: Donation history (Amount, Date, Campaign).

## 3. Purpose of Processing
Your data is processed for the following purposes:
- Executing donation transactions.
- Verifying identity for legal compliance.
- Keeping records of donations and issuing receipts.
- Ensuring platform security and preventing fraud.

## 4. Transfer of Data
Your personal data is not shared with third parties, except:
- **Legal Authorities**: Upon official request or as required by law.
- **Payment Processors**: Necessary data is shared with licensed payment intermediaries (e.g., Iyzico) to process payments securely.

## 5. Data Security Measures
We take technical and administrative measures to protect your data, including:
- Encryption of sensitive data (AES-256).
- Access restrictions based on roles.
- Regular security audits and logging.

## 6. Your Rights
Under Article 11 of KVKK, you have the right to:
- Learn whether your personal data is processed.
- Request information if your personal data has been processed.
- Learn the purpose of processing and whether they are used appropriately.
- Request correction of incomplete or incorrect data.
- Request deletion or destruction of your personal data (`/api/v1/users/me` endpoint handles account deletion requests).
- Request notification of third parties to whom data has been transferred.
- Object to the occurrence of a result against yourself by analyzing the processed data exclusively through automated systems.
- Request compensation for damages in case of damage due to unlawful processing of personal data.

## 7. Contact
For your requests regarding your rights, please contact us via the platform or email `kvkk@seffafbagis.org`.
